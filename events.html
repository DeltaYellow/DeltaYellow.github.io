Veterans TSA Events

State Leadership Conference - April 13, 2016

After taking home more than 3 regional championship trophies, state competition has come to challenge our school's titles! Congratulations to those who have advanced to the state competition. We will be traveling from the BISD Main Building the morning of Wednesday, the 13th. Be ready with your projects and bring in your permission slips ASAP! We are excited to go for the gold once again at the state competition! Remember to be there promptly at 5 in the morning, as we will be traveling for quite a few hours.

Regional Competition - February 27, 2016

The time has finally come! The Regional TSA Competition will be held at the Science Academy of South Texas on March 4-5. Certain projects require early online submission this year, so check with Mr. Rodriguez if your project must be submitted early. Be sure to pick up a permission slip for the competition by next week. In case you are driving to the competition, check directions early and make sure you arrive on time!

Directions to Sci-Tech >

Regional Leadership Conference - January 22, 2016

The annual Regional Leadership Conference was held at UTRGV with huge success! Veterans TSA members Aleida Olvera and Jaime Martinez helped lead the conference as state officers. Heriberto Reynoso was invited as a guest to speak about his work with robotics.

Visit Mr. Reynoso's Robotics >

Kerrville Statewide Leadership Conference - December 9, 2015

Kerrville was a blast! For the first time, more underclassmen than upperclassmen from Veterans TSA attended the event. Jaime and Aleida helped put together the conference and did an astounding job. What a great way to introduce the underclassmen to TSA.

Upcoming Trip - November 21, 2015

Kerrville is around the corner. In just over a week, 20 members of our chapter will be travelling to Kerrville, TX for the annual Texas TSA Leadership Conference. Those who will be attending must turn in permission slips by next Wednesday and report to Sams Stadium by 6:30 AM on Thursday. Don't forget to bring a jacket because it can get very cold in Kerrville.

Learn more about Kerrville >

Boo at the Zoo! - November 1, 2015

Thanks to everyone who came out to help the past nights. Boo at the Zoo was a success, our costumes were both scary and funny and a hit with the crowd. Those who need a certificate or signature for volunteer hours should contact Mr. Villarreal.
